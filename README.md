# Business_Insights_360


#Situation

AtliQ Hardware, a fast-growing consumer electronics company operating globally, faced increasing business complexity due to expansion across markets, products, and sales channels. The company sells hardware products through multiple channels such as retailers, distributors, and direct platforms.

Management relied heavily on Excel files for reporting and analysis, but as data volume increased, Excel became inefficient for handling large-scale analytics and decision-making.

#Task

The objective was to build a scalable analytics solution that could:

Replace manual Excel-based reporting
Consolidate data from multiple business functions
Enable stakeholders to make faster, data-driven decisions
Provide visibility into sales performance, finance, marketing, Supply Chain, and product insights

#Action

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to transform raw business data into meaningful insights.

Key activities included:

Imported and transformed data using Power Query
Built a robust data model connecting multiple tables
Created DAX measures for KPI calculations and business metrics
Designed dynamic dashboards for:
Finance
Sales
Marketing
Supply Chain
Executive
Implemented filters, slicers, and drill-through features for better user interaction
Optimized dashboard performance and report usability

Result

The solution enabled:

✔ Faster and more efficient reporting compared to Excel workflows
✔ Improved visibility into business performance across departments
✔ Data-driven decision making for management and stakeholders
✔ Centralized reporting through interactive and scalable dashboards
